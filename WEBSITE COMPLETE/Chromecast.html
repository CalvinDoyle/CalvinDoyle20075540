Google Chromecast, future of media streaming

Chromecast is a line of digital media players developed by Google.
Designed as small dongles, the devices play audio/video content on a high-definition television or home audio system by directly streaming it via Wi-Fi from the Internet or a local network.
They come at very affordable prices, the lowest being 35 euro and the most expensive being 60 euro.
Both of which are very high quality devices, only difference between the two is the higher 4K definition on the more expensive version
