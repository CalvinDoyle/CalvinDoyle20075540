Android Smart Watches

Android Smart Watches are like a companion to your phone and in some cases take over the role of a phone quite well.
They feature always on displays so you'll always see messages/notifications etc. Some features don't even require your phone to be near you! As long as you have Wi-Fi that is.
They can also function as a pedometer and have many fitness apps available to assist your exercise regime.
Their high level of customization is probably their strongest suit
