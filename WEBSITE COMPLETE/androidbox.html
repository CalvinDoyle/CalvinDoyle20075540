Android TV box

Android TV boxes are essentially like your set top box you get from Sky or UPC, you can use them to watch TV and access all the channels you normally could,
the difference is these are like a smart TV on a budget. They hover around the 70 euro price range and are great value for money,
they can run pretty much any app for whatever purpose, such as film streaming or even games.
The fact that they are incredibly small is a big factor too, most are not even 5 inches wide!
